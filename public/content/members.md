## Principal Investigator

### Hong Huang
**Position**: PhD Student  
**Institution**: City University of Hong Kong  
**Image**: /image/HongHuang.jpg  
**Bio**: Hong Huang received the BE degree in computer science and technology from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, in 2021, and the MS degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Florida, Gainesville, USA, in 2023. He is a Ph.D. student with the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong SAR. He has served as a reviewer for several prestigious journals and conferences, including NeurIPS, AAAI, ICLR, IEEE TPDS, IEEE TNNLS, etc. He has authored 8 scientific papers in international journals and conference proceedings, such as NeurIPS, CVPR, ACL, etc. His research interests mainly include model compression, edge computing, and distributed learning.
